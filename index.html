The Wines!

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Dealu Mare region, amidst sun-kissed rolling hills, lies Budureasca vineyards—a place steeped in history and deeply rooted in the cultivation of grapes. The origins of winemaking in this region trace back to ancient times, with evidence suggesting a tradition that dates as far back as the Dacians and even their ancestors, the Thracians. We will also delve into the remarkable Budureasca Premium Fume and the exquisite Budureasca Sauvignon & Sauvignon, two exceptional wines that exemplify the winemaker's commitment to quality and heritage.
